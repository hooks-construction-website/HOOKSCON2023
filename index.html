SERVICES

Residential Remodel

A Hooks Construction specialty, we focus on producing an end result that looks like it was part of the whole from the beginning. ABOUT US

Founded in 2006, Hooks Construction is a full-service builder and designer dedicated to integrating clients' needs into a beautiful and functional structure. The company's founder Matt Hooks grew up with a hammer in his hand and learned the trades from his father. 5 years of art school helped refine his eye for detail, and a lifetime of experience has honed his skills in design and building.

Decades of Experience in Building

We bring decades of experience to the table an pride ourselves in the quality of our craft. While we enjoy all aspects of building, from new construction to remodeling, our specialty is residential remodel. We strive to produce an end result that looks like it was part of the whole from the beginning. We are happy to work with your plan, whether it's drawings on a napkin or fully prepared blueprints. We can bring your vision to life.
